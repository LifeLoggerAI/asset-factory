## Prohibited conclusions

The presence of these files does not prove:

- provider-backed video generation;
- paid provider authorization or spend;
- final cinematic footage;
- production persistence or personal-memory storage;
- medical or safety outcomes;
- certified AR, VR or XR hardware integration;
- public campaign approval.

## Current evidence classes

- `LIVE_VERIFIED`: directly supported by the certified deployed Spatial SHA and retained release evidence.
- `WORKING_DEMO`: source/sample behavior that has executable evidence but is not a certified persistent service.
- `PROTOTYPE`: implemented candidate that is not approved or deployed.
- `VISION`: future capability or narrative framing only.

Every shot must name one evidence class and its exact evidence reference. Mixed-class shots must use the weakest applicable class.

## Publication boundary

Publication requires separate human approval for creative, product truth, privacy, claims, accessibility, rights and final release. Paid generation additionally requires a separate immutable authorization containing exact source SHA, provider/model, maximum calls and absolute spend ceiling.

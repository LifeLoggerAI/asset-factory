# Anchor Film Order

The first production tranche is intentionally limited to six anchor films so continuity can be solved before generating the full campaign.

1. Day 0 — The Doorway Exists
2. Day 1 — Movies of Life
3. Day 2 — This Is Real
4. Day 7 — Ask Me First
5. Day 8 — Life Becomes a Place
6. Day 14 — The World Opens

After these six pass creative, product-truth, privacy, accessibility, and technical quality review, their canon becomes the reference for Days 3-6 and Days 9-13.

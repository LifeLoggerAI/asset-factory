## Audio and accessibility

- Dialogue must remain understandable without music.
- Captions must be burned into social cuts and supplied separately as SRT and WebVTT.
- Audio description must identify route changes, orb movement and the transition into Replay.
- Reduced-motion cut must use dissolves rather than rapid camera flight.
- No flashing sequence above three flashes per second.

## Rejection conditions

Reject the film if it implies medical benefit, real personal-memory storage, autonomous provider activity, certified device integration, global operational scale, or public approval not supported by an exact receipt.

# Waiting Room Quality Gates

A film or social cut is blocked unless every applicable gate is explicitly passed.

## Product truth

- Every shot has one evidence class and exact source receipt.
- `LIVE_VERIFIED` footage matches the exact deployed SHA.
- Sample memories and reconstructions are disclosed.
- No shot implies persistent personal data, active providers, medical outcomes or certified devices without separate evidence.

## Visual quality

- No duplicate or stale route owner is visible.
- Camera motion is stable and intentional.
- UI text is legible at delivery resolution.
- Desktop, 9:16 and 1:1 crops are independently reviewed.
- No broken asset, missing font, blank WebGL frame or fallback failure appears.
- Continuity of orb, environment, lighting and interface state is maintained.

## Accessibility

- Accurate captions and transcript exist.
- Audio-description cues exist for all essential visual changes.
- Reduced-motion edit exists.
- Contrast and disclosure reading time are sufficient.
- No unsafe flashing or rapid strobing.

## Privacy, rights and safety

- No real private user data appears.
- Names, faces, locations, music, voices and third-party artwork have documented rights or are synthetic/sample assets.
- Screenshots contain no secrets, tokens, internal URLs or unredacted diagnostics.
- Medical, child-safety, crisis, financial and legal implications receive specific review before use.

## Technical delivery

- Master and social cuts decode successfully.
- Audio channels and loudness are verified.
- Captions align with speech.
- File hashes, dimensions, duration and codecs are recorded.
- Fallback stills and poster frames are retained.

## Human approvals

Required approvers are creative, product truth, privacy, claims, accessibility, rights and final publish. A paid-provider film additionally requires a separate immutable spend authorization and provider-transaction receipt.

No approval may be inferred from CI, file existence, generated previews or the author’s own review.

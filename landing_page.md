## How It Works

1.  **Define Your Asset:** Submit a structured request to our API defining the asset you need.
2.  **Deterministic Generation:** Our version-locked pipeline generates a complete media kit with guaranteed repeatability.
3.  **Receive Your Manifest:** Get a detailed manifest with file hashes and URLs for every generated asset.
4.  **Integrate and Scale:** Use the assets programmatically in your applications, websites, and workflows.

# 12-Month Revenue Execution Plan\n\n**Objective:** Achieve \$1M in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) within 12 months.\n\n## Phase 1: Months 1-3 - Founder-Led Sales & Early Adopters\n\n-   **Target:** 10 early adopter clients on the \"Agency\" plan.\n-   **Price Point:** \$1,000/month.\n-   **Strategy:**\n    -   Direct outreach to marketing agencies, content creators, and businesses with high-volume content needs.\n    -   Focus on the \"programmable media infrastructure\" angle.\n    -   Offer a high-touch onboarding experience.\n-   **Goal:** Validate the product and pricing, gather testimonials, and generate initial revenue.\n\n## Phase 2: Months 4-6 - Inbound Marketing & Self-Service\n\n-   **Target:** 100 customers on the \"Pro\" and \"Agency\" plans.\n-   **Price Points:** \$200/month (Pro), \$1,000/month (Agency).\n-   **Strategy:**\n    -   Launch the full enterprise landing page.\n    -   Publish technical tutorials and use-case articles.\n    -   Implement a self-service sign-up and onboarding flow.\n    -   Invest in targeted ads on developer-focused platforms.\n-   **Goal:** Build a scalable customer acquisition funnel and grow the customer base.\n\n## Phase 3: Months 7-9 - Enterprise Sales & Strategic Partnerships\n\n-   **Target:** 5 enterprise clients.\n-   **Price Point:** \$5,000+/month (custom pricing).\n-   **Strategy:**\n    -   Hire a dedicated enterprise salesperson.\n    -   Develop case studies from early adopters.\n    -   Target companies with a clear need for media automation at scale.\n    -   Explore partnerships with complementary technology providers (e.g., marketing automation platforms).\n-   **Goal:** Land high-value, long-term contracts and establish a foothold in the enterprise market.\n\n## Phase 4: Months 10-12 - Expansion & Optimization\n\n-   **Target:** Double the number of customers in all segments.\n-   **Strategy:**\n    -   Optimize the marketing funnel based on data.\n    -   Introduce new features and capabilities based on customer feedback.\n    -   Expand the sales team.\n    -   Host webinars and workshops to educate the market.\n-   **Goal:** Accelerate growth and solidify Asset Factory\'s position as the leading programmable media infrastructure.\n\n---\n\n**Disclaimer:** This is a high-level plan and should be adapted based on market feedback and business performance.\n
